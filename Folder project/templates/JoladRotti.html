Ingredients

  • 2 cups Jowar flour (sorghum flour)
  • Salt to taste
  • Hot water as needed

Jolad Rotti Recipe

  • 1In a bowl, mix 2 cups of jowar flour with salt to taste.
  • 2Add hot water little by little and knead into a soft dough.
  • 3Divide the dough into small balls.
  • 4On a flat surface, roll each ball into a thin, round rotti using a rolling pin or patting it by hand.
  • 5Heat a tawa (griddle) and cook the rotti on medium heat.
  • 6Cook both sides until light brown spots appear. No need to use oil.
  • 7Remove from heat and serve hot with chutney or curry.
