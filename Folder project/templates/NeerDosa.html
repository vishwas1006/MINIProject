Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Neer Dosa Recipe

  • 1Soak 1 cup of rice in water for 4-5 hours.
  • 2Grind the soaked rice with enough water to make a smooth batter.
  • 3Add salt to taste and mix well. The batter should be very thin and watery.
  • 4Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with oil.
  • 5Pour a ladleful of batter and swirl the pan to spread it evenly.
  • 6Cook until the edges start to lift. No need to flip.
  • 7Fold the dosa and remove it from the pan. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  • 8Serve hot with coconut chutney or any side dish of your choice.
