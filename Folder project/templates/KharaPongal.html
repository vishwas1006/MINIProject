Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1/2 cup Moong dal (split yellow gram)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp Black peppercorns
  • 1/4 tsp Asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • A few Curry leaves
  • 1 inch Ginger, grated
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 cups Water

Khara Pongal Recipe

  • 1Dry roast 1/2 cup of moong dal until it turns light brown. Rinse it with water.
  • 2In a pressure cooker, add 1 cup of rice, roasted moong dal, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, and 8 cups of water.
  • 3Pressure cook for 4-5 whistles until the rice and dal are cooked and mushy.
  • 4Heat 2 tbsp of ghee in a pan. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 tsp black peppercorns, and a pinch of asafoetida.
  • 5Add a few curry leaves and 1 inch grated ginger. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  • 6Add the cooked rice and dal mixture to the pan. Mix well.
  • 7Add salt to taste and cook on low heat for a few minutes.
  • 8Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
