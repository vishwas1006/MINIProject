Ingredients

  • 1 cup Ragi flour (finger millet flour)
  • 2 cups Water
  • Salt to taste

Ragi Mudde Recipe

  • 1In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add salt to taste.
  • 2Mix 1 tablespoon of ragi flour in 1/4 cup water to make a paste.
  • 3Add the ragi paste to the boiling water and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  • 4Slowly add the remaining ragi flour while stirring continuously.
  • 5Reduce heat to low and cover the pan. Cook for 5-10 minutes.
  • 6Stir well until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  • 7Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  • 8Wet your hands with water, take a portion of the mixture, and shape it into balls (mudde).
  • 9Serve hot with sambar, curry, or yogurt.
