Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1 cup Coconut (grated)
  • 2 cups Jaggery
  • 1/4 cup Ghee
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup Cashew nuts
  • 1/4 cup Raisins

Finger Millet Halwa Recipe

  • 1Soak 1 cup of rice in water for 3-4 hours.
  • 2Grind the soaked rice along with 1 cup of grated coconut to a fine paste using some water.
  • 3In a heavy-bottomed pan, dissolve 2 cups of jaggery in 2 cups of water. Strain to remove impurities.
  • 4Add the ground rice-coconut paste to the jaggery syrup and mix well.
  • 5Cook the mixture on medium heat, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  • 6Add 1/4 cup ghee in small increments while continuing to stir.
  • 7Cook until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  • 8Add 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder and mix well.
  • 9In a separate pan, fry 1/4 cup cashew nuts and 1/4 cup raisins in a little ghee until golden brown.
  • 10Add the fried cashew nuts and raisins to the cooked mixture and mix well.
  • 11Transfer the mixture to a greased plate and flatten it evenly.
  • 12Let it cool completely, then cut into desired shapes and serve.
