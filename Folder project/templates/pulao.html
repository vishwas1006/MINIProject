Pulao Recipe

  • 1Rinse 1 cup Basmati rice until water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • 2Cut 1 cup mixed vegetables into small pieces.
  • 3Heat 2 tablespoons oil or ghee in a pot over medium heat.
  • 4Add 1 bay leaf, 1 inch cinnamon stick, 3-4 cloves, and 2-3 cardamom pods. Sauté until fragrant.
  • 5Add 1 teaspoon cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • 6Add 1 sliced onion and sauté until golden brown. Add 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste and 2 slit green chilies, sauté for 1 minute.
  • 7Add 1 chopped tomato and cook until soft.
  • 8Add 1/2 teaspoon turmeric, 1 teaspoon garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  • 9Add rice, mix gently. Add 2 cups water, bring to a boil.
  • 10Reduce heat, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes until rice is cooked.
  • 11Let sit for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  • 12Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
