Bisibelebath Recipe

  • 1Rinse and soak 1 cup rice and 1/2 cup toor dal for 30 minutes.
  • 2Pressure cook rice and dal with 4 cups water (4-5 whistles).
  • 3Roast spices: 1 tbsp each of chana dal, urad dal, coriander seeds, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds, 1/2 tsp peppercorns, 4-5 dried red chilies.
  • 4Add 1/4 cup grated coconut, sauté, grind to paste.
  • 5Soak tamarind, extract pulp.
  • 6Sauté onion, add mixed vegetables.
  • 7Combine rice, dal, vegetables, spice paste, tamarind, salt.
  • 8Temper with ghee, mustard, cumin, asafoetida, curry leaves, red chilies.
  • 9Garnish with coriander, serve hot.
